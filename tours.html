Questions

Frequently asked questions

What's included in the tour price?+

Driver, fuel and mineral water are included. Entrance tickets and meals are not included.

Can I customize the itinerary?+

Yes, tell me what you want to see and I'll build a custom route for your day.

Do you speak English?+

Yes, I speak both English and Indonesian throughout the tour.

Is the vehicle air-conditioned?+

Yes, all vehicles are air-conditioned and cleaned before every trip.

What's your cancellation policy?+

Free rescheduling up to 24 hours before pickup. Details can be confirmed when you book.

Do you offer airport transfer?+

Yes, airport pickup and drop-off is available on request.

How many people can join one tour?+

Standard cars fit up to 6 people, larger groups can request the Hiace van.

Is it safe to travel with a private driver?+

Yes, I'm an experienced local driver who has safely hosted 500+ travelers.

What areas do you pick up from?+

Pickup and drop-off across South and Central Bali, including Kuta, Seminyak, Canggu, Ubud and Nusa Dua.

How do I pay?+

Cash, bank transfer, or a deposit in advance, depending on the tour.
