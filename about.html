The story behind MyBroBali

Not a company, not a call center — just one local driver who wants your Bali trip to feel effortless.

Meet your driver

Born in Bali, still showing my island to new friends every week

I'm Komang, born and raised in Bali. For over 8 years I've had the privilege of showing travelers from all over the world the Bali I grew up in — not just the famous spots, but the quiet corners locals actually love.

What started as helping a few friends find their way around has grown into a small, personal tour business built on one simple idea: treat every guest like family. No rushed itineraries, no pushy souvenir stops, just an honest, comfortable trip at your own pace.

My goal is simple: make sure you leave Bali with great memories, not travel stress.
